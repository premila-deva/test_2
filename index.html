Jobs

If you want an exciting career with challenge and opportunity, join our inclusive policing family and support us while we support you in delivering even safer communities for the whole of Cheshire.

Contact our recruitment teams

Please read the information on the relevant jobs pages, if you can't find the information you are looking for please contact one of our recruitment teams. 

Contact the police officer recruitment team at Recruitmentpoliceofficer@scotland.police.uk

Contact the police staff recruitment team at RecruitmentCentre@scotland.police.uk

Contact the transeferee/rejoiner recruitment team at RecruitmentTransferee@scotland.police.uk

Contact the PSYV recruitment team at volunteercoordination@scotland.police.uk

Contact the special constable recruitment team at Recruitmentspecialconstable@scotland.police.uk
